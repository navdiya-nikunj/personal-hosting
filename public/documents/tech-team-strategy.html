II. Tech Team Workflow & Operations

We operate on principles of efficiency and proactive support, leveraging automation and structured processes to drive results.

  • Infrastructure and Security: Daily management of Google Workspace, email security, Twilio numbers, and technical compliance.
  • Tool Support: Primary technical point of contact for third-party tools, providing troubleshooting, access, and guidance.
  • Digital Marketing Cycle: The **Social Media Manager** creates organic and paid content which feeds into campaigns run by the **Meta Ads Expert**. The **Website Developer** rapidly deploys and integrates corresponding landing pages.
  • Lead Nurturing Automations: Developing automated email and CRM sequences to guide leads toward qualification.
  • Sales Productivity: Implementing automations requested by the Sales Team to reduce manual tasks like data synchronization.
  • AI Integration: Deploying AI tools for enhanced lead qualification and personalized communication to improve conversion rates.

Request Management Protocol

All non-critical requests must adhere to this structured approach to ensure proper prioritization and efficient resource management.

Level 1 (Immediate Halt)

Critical Failure / Security Breach

Channel: Immediate Chat/Call

Level 2 (Standard Support)

Tool Usage / Minor Bug

Channel: Support Channel (e.g., Slack)

Level 3 (Scheduled Development)

New Automation / Feature / Website Change

Channel: Project Management System

III. Team Integration and Collaboration

We integrate with all departments by establishing foundational systems and then supporting ongoing process improvements.

Integration with Sales Team

Our focus is to provide the Sales Team with high-quality leads and streamlined workflows.

  • 1.System Setup: Ensuring CRM, lead capture, and communication systems are correctly configured to support the sales process from the start.

Integration with Legal Team

Our focus is on ensuring data integrity and technical compliance across all platforms.
